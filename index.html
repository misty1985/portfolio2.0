Bio


I am currently a student in the UC Davis coding bootcamp. I grew up in the Bay Area and moved to Sacramento in 2017. Before I started learning code I was a nanny; I left high school early but didn’t have a clear life-plan, so I began taking community college courses and continued nannying full time. I enjoyed the creative aspects of cutting and styling hair and enrolled in cosmetology school, but it wasn’t quite right for me. In the back of my mind, I had always considered coding and web design as my mom was in the field of web development. Over time I gained confidence to switch into the field. It’s been a very fun and much needed change. Hope you enjoy!
